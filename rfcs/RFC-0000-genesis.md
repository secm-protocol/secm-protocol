## 1. What is SECM?

SECM (System of Multilayer Knowledge Engineering) is NOT:

- a numerology system;
- an astrology system;
- a personality test;
- an AI assistant;
- a recommendation engine;
- a chatbot;
- a prediction engine.

SECM is a **Knowledge Engineering Protocol**.

Its purpose is to transform heterogeneous representations of reality into structured metadata, allowing multiple independent transformation engines to converge toward explainable directional hypotheses that reduce uncertainty in human decision making.

The protocol is independent from any AI model.

GPT is not SECM. Codex is not SECM. Future AI models are not SECM.

They are interchangeable computational workers.

The protocol is permanent.

## 2. Core Mission

The mission of SECM is:

**Reduce the waste of human potential through explainable knowledge convergence.**

The protocol exists to help people, organizations and societies better understand:

- themselves;
- their context;
- their trajectory;
- their environment;
- their constraints;
- their opportunities;
- their probable directions.

The protocol never determines destiny. It estimates directions.

## 3. Philosophy

SECM does not inherit disciplines. SECM decomposes disciplines.

Historical systems, mathematical systems, symbolic systems, behavioral systems, economic systems, scientific systems and future systems are never adopted as beliefs.

They are decomposed into computational transformations.

The protocol never asks: *"Do we believe this?"*

It asks: *"What transformation does this model perform?"*

Every model becomes:

```
Input → Transformation → Metadata → Convergence → Directional Estimation
```
